About

I suppose a few questions need a few answers. What is this, who am I, what qualifies me to speak on this topic, how did I get into your house, etcetera.

Well, my dearest reader, I'm Neko. I'm a hacker working in network security. I've also had a very rough experience with existence so far. For now, I'll save you the details. I've been seeing a psychiatrist and a therapist in an effort to address my personal traumas. My therapist has been giving me a myriad of resources, and, with my busy life, it's become difficult to keep track of them. I created this website to do that for me and also to share those resources, my thoughts on them, and some of my personal experiences. I hope we find some value in this, and thank you for reading.
